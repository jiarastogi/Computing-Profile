My Future Aspirations

In the future, I want to continue developing my computing skills, which will allow me to apply them in creative, practical, and professional ways. My past experiences have shown me that computing is a broad and valuable field that can be used across many industries for different purposes. In the future, I hope to create a business that helps people, while using computing skills to improve and systemise its processes. No matter the industry, computing can play an important role in improving efficiency, communication, and overall success.

Future Goals, Specifically in Computing

I'm interested in improving my skills of programming and digital development. Since I already have built a foundation of creating websites and landing pages, improving these skills will help me stand out and add features that bring value to customers and the users. Additionally, following my Bachelor of AI, I want to be more involved in learning how AI works and how I can apply that in a business perspective, especially to improve the processes of the business and reducing repetitive tasks.

How Computing Could Support My Career

I believe computing will continue to be an important part of my future because technology is constantly changing the way people live and work. With increased uses of AI, it forces people to learn new ways of working and living to integrate the power technologies into their day-to-day activities. Whether I use computing for web development, business productivity, digital design, or in a completely different area, I want to keep building the skills needed to adapt to new technologies and use them effectively.

Possible Future Pathways
